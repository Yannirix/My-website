About Me

Hi there! My name is Yanni, I'm 12 years old and have been game developing for 6 and a half months. I absolutely love it! Every project is a fun challenge, and I get to learn something new every day.

I enjoy making all kinds of games, from spooky horror adventures to fun platformers and cool simulations. My goal is to create awesome worlds where players can dive in and have a blast! I get inspired by all sorts of games and try to add my own twist to everything I create.

Right now, I'm all about combining great stories with fun gameplay. I think a good story makes a game even more exciting and keeps players coming back for more!

I’m always looking to improve my skills, whether it’s learning new stuff in Godot or just getting better at coding. I love connecting with other game creators and sharing ideas!

If you have any tips or want to chat, please feel free to reach out!
